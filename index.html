podCOIN, puts volunteer hours on the blockchain — a public ledger that tracks who works for which policies, which candidates back them, and which proposed legislation moves them forward.

See the policies → How it works
i.

Volunteer hours, on-chain

Every hour spent promoting a policy gets recorded to the blockchain ledger. Verifiable. Permanent. Yours.

ii.

Policies, not parties

Policies are the unit of work. Each one carries its own page, supporters, and the candidates and legislation that align with it.

iii.

Public, sortable, accountable

Anyone can see which policies have momentum, which candidates back them, and how the public is voting in real time.
